The final database, tables and collections.

Our Final Entity-Relationship Diagram

SQL was used to build the database. We chose this option as our data had a structured format. Every clinical trial had a defined ID, with information on disease conditions being studied, types of interventions used, companies involved, trial results posted dates, etc. The companies also had a ticker associated, and each company had stock price values for a given time-period. Each of the datasets could be setup in predefined schemas and relationships between the tables established using primary and foreign keys, making it a optimal and safe choice for making queries in this huge database.
